[English]
The app will automatically attempt to provide the closest matching word, starting with your input.
[Enter] Finds the closest word in the dictionary that matches your input. Useful for overlooking spelling mistakes.
[Shift + Enter] Finds the closest word in the thesaurus that matches your input. This only works for English words and the result is always in English. Keep in mind this sends an external request, so it requires a network connection.
